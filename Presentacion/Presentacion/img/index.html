{#}what we do

RubyOnRails Software Factory

We will help you from the conception of your idea to building and maintaining it as a working software. UI, UX, graphic design, back-end development, front-end development. We are on it!

{#}how we do it

Agile

We use Agile methods all the way. Agile inception, Agile planning, Scrum, XP, pair programming, retrospectives, agile project management. That way we can adapt to your every need.

Let's chat

Tell us about your idea. We want to know how we can help you. We'll have a session of agile inception.

Prototype

We sketch and wireframe your application. Then lay it out and discuss it with you. After this, you’ll be able to visualize your future application.

Create

We work with you through Project Management Sofware. You can check the evolution of your project in real-time.

Creative

Our designers will put all of their creative skills at your service, to make your app/site stand out from the rest.
